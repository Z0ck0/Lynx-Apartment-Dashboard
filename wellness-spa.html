Wellness & Spa in Cevahir Complex

Guests at Lynx Apartment have access to premium wellness facilities inside the same complex. These services are not included in the apartment price and are paid separately at the spa reception.

💳 Pricing

  • Gym – 300 MKD (~5 EUR) per day or 1,500 MKD (~25 EUR) per week
  • Pool, Spa & Sauna – 900 MKD (~15 EUR) per day or 2,500 MKD (~40 EUR) per week
  • Combined Gym + Spa Weekly Package – 3,000 MKD (~49 EUR)

🏊 Important Notes

  • Swimming cap is required for the pool (available on-site for 300 MKD)
  • Availability and access are managed by the building's spa reception

Important Notice

The wellness and spa facilities are operated independently by the Cevahir Complex. Lynx Apartment and your host are not responsible for facility availability, pricing changes, or service quality. Please contact the spa reception directly for current rates and availability.
