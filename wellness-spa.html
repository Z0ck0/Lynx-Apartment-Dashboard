💳 Pricing
All services must be paid directly at the spa reception:

• Gym – 300 MKD (~5 EUR) per day or 1,500 MKD (~25 EUR) per week
• Pool, Spa & Sauna – 900 MKD (~15 EUR) per day or 2,500 MKD (~40 EUR) per week
• Combined Gym + Spa Weekly Package – 3,000 MKD (~49 EUR)
🏊 Important Notes
• Swimming cap is required for the pool (available on-site for 300 MKD)
• Availability and access are managed by the building's spa reception
• Contact the spa reception directly for current rates and availability
