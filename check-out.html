Check-in & Checkout Instructions

Smooth, easy, and secure self check-in & check-out at

Lynx Apartment

Check-in instructions
🚪
Enter the mailbox room
Enter the mailbox room located across from the reception desk in the lobby.
🔍
Find mailbox 109
Locate mailbox 109 on the wall - it's on the top row, 6th mailbox from the right, directly in front as you enter.
🔐
Enter combination code
Please contact your host for the combination lock code. Make sure the digits are perfectly aligned with the center line.
↩️
Turn left to open
Once the correct combination is entered, gently turn the lock counterclockwise (to the left) to open the mailbox.
🔑
Retrieve your key
Take your apartment key from inside the mailbox. If you have any issues, please contact your host.

Checkout instructions
🧺
Gather used towels
Please place all used towels in the bathroom or in the laundry basket provided.
🗑️
Throw trash away
Kindly dispose of trash in the bin located on your apartment floor.
⏲️
Turn things off
Before leaving, turn off lights, air conditioning, heating, boiler, and any used appliances.
🔒
Lock up
Close all windows and lock the apartment door securely before checking out.
🔑
Return keys
Return all keys and access tags (elevator & car ramp) to the designated spot. A €50 fee applies for any lost or damaged items.
📝
Additional requests
• Load any used dishes into the dishwasher or wash them by hand.
• Check out by 11:00 AM unless otherwise agreed.
• Message us once you've checked out. Safe travels!
🔑 Self Check-Out Instructions
  • Return the Key:
    • Go to the mailbox area just across from the reception desk in the lobby.
    • Find Mailbox 109 — top row, 6th from the right, directly in front as you enter the mailbox room.
    • Drop the key through the slot — no code needed.
