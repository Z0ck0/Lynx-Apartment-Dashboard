🔑 Self Check-In (Combination Lock)
Step 1: Enter the mailbox room across from reception
Step 2: Find mailbox 109 on the wall (top row, 6th from right)
Step 3: Enter combination 050 and turn left to open

To access your key, please follow these steps:

📍 Locate the Mailbox

  • Find Mailbox 109 in the building's mailbox area — located just across from the reception desk in the lobby.
  • The Mailbox 109 is directly in front of you as you enter the mailbox room, on the top row, 6th mailbox from the right.

🔐 Open the Lock

  1. Enter the code: Set the numbers to 0 - 5 - 0 on the lock.
    • Make sure the digits are perfectly aligned with the center line on the dial.
  2. Turn the dial counterclockwise (to the left):
    Once the correct combination is entered, gently turn the lock to the left (opposite of clock direction).
  3. If the lock turns, the code is correct — go ahead and open the mailbox door.
  4. If the lock does NOT turn:
    • The combination may not be precisely aligned.
    • Please realign the digits carefully and try turning left again.
  5. Take your apartment key from inside the mailbox.

Checkout instructions

Gather used towels

🗑️

Kindly dispose of trash in the bin located on your apartment floor.
⏲️
Turn things off
Before leaving, turn off lights, air conditioning, heating, boiler, and any used appliances.
🔒
Lock up
Close all windows and lock the apartment door securely before checking out.
🔑
Return keys
Return all keys and access tags (elevator & car ramp) to the designated spot. A €50 fee applies for any lost or damaged items.
📝
Additional requests
• Load any used dishes into the dishwasher or wash them by hand.

• Message us once you've checked out. Safe travels!
